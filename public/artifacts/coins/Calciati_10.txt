  right wearing a mustache / Crab; conch below. Jenkins, "Coins of Punic
  Sicily," in SNR 50 (1971), pl. 23, 14; SNG ANS 510; Calciati I pg.
  278, 10; BMC Sicily -; SNG Copenhagen -; SNG Morcom -; Laffaille -;
